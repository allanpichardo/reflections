Instructions

  • Use the reflections slider to adjust the maximum number of bounces off of the mirrors 🟦
  • Drag the object 🔺 and the eye ⚫️ to the desired location
  • Hover over a mirror surface 🔺 to see a potential reflection point
  • Click a reflection point to cast a light ray from the object 🔺 to the eye ⚫
  • If the light strikes the eye, the object's reflection will appear
